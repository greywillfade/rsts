Records Sound the Same was formed in 2014

We originally focused on discovery and planning for websites - uncovering user and business needs, potential opportunities, and what these meant for design and technology selection.

Since then, whilst planning websites was still something that people needed, we found our most successful projects focused on looking beyond the browser, as a driver for more fundamental transformation and benefits.

Thinking beyond websites

We looked beyond just websites, and instead took a holistic view of working with the web to help make meaningful change happen for the people we worked with — whether across systems and products, processes, strategy, or culture.

We were an independent, technology agnostic, embedded partner who could understand specific situations as well as helping to give recommendations and skills to transform how you work.
