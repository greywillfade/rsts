Get in touch

Whether you’d like to discuss a project, have a question you’d need answering, or simply want find out more, please drop us a message.

Alternatively, if you'd like to stay up to date, you can also join the mailing list.

Sending a note to introduce yourself is the best way to kick off a project. We can then either arrange a call or video chat, or meet up somewhere.

The Records Sound the Same office is in Sudbury in Suffolk, but we’re often out and about around the UK (and further afield) so are happy to come to you.
